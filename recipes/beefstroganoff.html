Beef Stroganoff Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound beef sirloin, sliced into thin strips
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 8 ounces sliced mushrooms
  • 1 can (10.5 oz) condensed beef broth
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 12 oz egg noodles, cooked and drained

Directions:

  1. Combine flour, salt, and pepper in a shallow dish. Dredge beef strips in flour mixture.
  2. Melt 2 tablespoons of butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add beef strips and cook until browned on all sides. Remove beef from skillet and set aside.
  3. Melt remaining 2 tablespoons of butter in the same skillet. Add onion and garlic, and cook until tender. Add mushrooms and cook until they release their liquid and it evaporates.
  4. Stir in beef broth, scraping the bottom of the skillet to loosen any browned bits. Return beef strips to skillet and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 10 minutes, or until sauce has thickened slightly.
  5. Remove skillet from heat and stir in sour cream and parsley. Serve beef stroganoff over cooked egg noodles.

Spicy Tofu Stir-Fry

    Home

Ingredients

  • 1 block of firm tofu
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 small onion, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp grated ginger
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp hoisin sauce
  • 1 tsp brown sugar
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Remove tofu from packaging and press out excess water. Cut into 1-inch cubes and set aside.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over high heat. Add the sliced bell peppers and onion, and cook for 2-3 minutes until they start to soften.
  3. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger, and cook for another 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  4. Add the cubed tofu to the skillet, and cook for 2-3 minutes until lightly browned.
  5. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, hoisin sauce, brown sugar, red pepper flakes, salt, and black pepper. Pour the sauce over the tofu and vegetables, and stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes until the sauce has thickened and everything is well coated.
  6. Garnish with chopped green onions, and serve hot with rice or noodles.
